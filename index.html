Dangers
Overdose symptoms:
  • Slow, weak, or no breathing
  • Drowsiness / Dizziness
  • Nausea / Vomiting
  • Pinpoint pupils
  • Disorientation
  • Choking or gurgling
  • Limp body
  • Discolored skin (lips and nails)
  • Cold and clammy skin
